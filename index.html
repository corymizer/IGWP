IGWP - In God We Play


About Us

IN GOD WE PLAY is a Lifestyle Brand, centered around providing engaging environments & collaborative mentorship through the vehicle of sports for both athletes and parents.

Our Mission

IGWP's mission is to bridge the generational faith gap by providing student athletes the tools they need for success at every turn of their careers, all in an effort to build character and maintain a moral standard.

Our Goal

Our goal is to leverage fruitful relationships in the business and industry to help provide opportunities in sport, education, entertainment, recreation, and entrepreneurial endeavors for the athletes and parents who need it most.

Our Motto

“In God We Play” was chosen as the motto to keep a daily reminder to not compromise the BLESSINGS from whom makes all things possible.

“Therefore encourage one another and build each other up, just as in fact you are doing.” 1 Thessalonians 5:11
